**Project name** \n
Reducing Greenhouse Gas Emissions at Home
**Short description**
Teaches users how to reduce GHG emissions in their home by making changes to different rooms in their house, and also tests users on their knowledge after the lesson 
**Problem statement** 
People want to make more sustainable decisions at home, but don't know how and don't know how expensive it will be
**Intended users**
Anyone interested in reducing GHG emissions by making changes at home
**Planned features**
Lesson plan + quiz
